Frequently Asked Questions

How difficult is installation?

Most homeowners can install our smart irrigation controller app in under 30 minutes. The system connects to your existing irrigation valves and requires only basic wiring knowledge. Detailed installation guides and video tutorials are provided, and our support team is always available to help.

Does it work with my existing system?

UVC-Smart is compatible with virtually all standard 24V AC irrigation systems. If you have traditional sprinkler valves, our controller can manage them. We support drip irrigation, spray systems, and rotary sprinklers across residential, commercial, and agricultural applications.

What if my internet goes down?

Our automated watering schedule system stores your schedules locally and continues operating even without internet connectivity. You'll lose remote access and weather integration temporarily, but your irrigation will run as programmed. Once connectivity is restored, all features resume automatically.

Can I control multiple properties?

Yes! Our remote irrigation system control platform supports unlimited properties under a single account. This makes it perfect for property managers, landscape professionals, and businesses managing multiple locations. Each property has independent schedules and settings.
