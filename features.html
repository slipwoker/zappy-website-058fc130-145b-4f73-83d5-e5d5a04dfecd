Powerful Features for Complete Remote Irrigation System Control

UVC-Smart's smart irrigation controller app combines advanced automation with intuitive controls, giving you unprecedented command over your watering systems from anywhere in the world.

Web-Based Dashboard

Access your complete irrigation system through any web browser. No app downloads required - just log in from your smartphone, tablet, or computer for instant remote irrigation system control.

Flexible Scheduling

Create custom watering schedules for each zone with our automated watering schedule system. Set different programs for weekdays and weekends, adjust by season, or let our smart algorithms optimize automatically.

Weather Intelligence

Integrated weather forecasting automatically adjusts your irrigation schedule. Skip watering when rain is expected, increase duration during heat waves, and optimize based on real-time conditions.

Multi-Zone Management

Control up to 32 separate irrigation zones independently. Each zone can have unique schedules, duration settings, and water flow rates tailored to specific landscape needs.

Instant Manual Override

Need to water right now? Start any zone instantly with manual override. Perfect for spot watering, testing your system, or handling unexpected situations.

Smart Notifications

Receive real-time alerts for system events, completed watering cycles, weather changes, and maintenance needs. Stay informed without constantly checking your dashboard.

Water Usage Analytics

Track your water consumption with detailed reports and visualizations. Identify trends, optimize usage, and see exactly how much you're saving with smart irrigation.

Multi-User Access

Share access with family members, property managers, or maintenance teams. Set custom permissions to control what each user can view and modify.

Plant-Specific Programs

Pre-configured watering programs for common plant types, lawn varieties, and landscape features. Simply select your plants and let the system optimize watering automatically.

Soil Moisture Integration

Connect optional soil moisture sensors for even more precise control. Water only when your soil actually needs it, preventing over-watering and under-watering.

Advanced Automation Features

Intelligent Scheduling Engine

Our automated watering schedule system learns from your preferences and local conditions. It automatically adjusts watering times, duration, and frequency based on weather patterns, seasonal changes, and historical data. The system can even predict your landscape's water needs days in advance.

Predictive Weather Adjustments

Using hyperlocal weather data and advanced forecasting, the system proactively modifies your irrigation schedule. It delays watering when rain is expected, increases duration during dry spells, and adjusts for temperature extremes - all without manual intervention.

Comprehensive Reporting

Access detailed analytics on water usage, system performance, and cost savings. Generate custom reports for any date range, compare zones, and track improvements over time. Perfect for property managers and budget-conscious homeowners.
